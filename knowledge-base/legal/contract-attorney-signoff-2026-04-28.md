## Seven TOLF Corrections Applied

The following corrections identified by Ottley Law Firm were applied to the contract template prior to submission:

1. **M.G.L. c. 142A §2 deposit limitation** — Explicit statutory cap language added to Article 3.1 (deposit not to exceed 1/3 of Contract Price or actual custom-material cost).
2. **Pass-through costs itemization** — Article 3.3 restructured to clearly distinguish contractor-advanced costs vs. owner-direct payments; pre-construction advances separately itemized on Invoice 1.
3. **No Change Order for code non-compliance** — Article 4.3 added: no Change Order may authorize work that violates 780 CMR or applicable law; such orders are void.
4. **780 CMR scope reference** — Article 1.1 updated to reference 780 CMR as in effect on date of permit application, including Massachusetts amendments, and NFPA 70.
5. **Arbitration / jury waiver disclosure** — Article 13.4 added mandatory notice to owner that agreeing to binding arbitration waives the right to jury trial; owner encouraged to consult an attorney.
6. **Massachusetts governing law clause** — Article 13.5 added explicit choice-of-law provision (Commonwealth of Massachusetts, no conflict-of-law principles).
7. **M.G.L. c. 254 §4 mechanics' lien notice** — Lien rights disclosure referenced in sub-contractor/supplier notice section per Massachusetts mechanics' lien statute.
